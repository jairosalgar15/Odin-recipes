Lasagna

Description

asagna (in Italian, lasagna) is a type of pasta. It is usually served in overlapping sheets interspersed with layers of ingredients to taste, most frequently meat (ragout) in bolognese and bechamel sauce. Its origin is Italian and the dish usually prepared with meat is usually called baked lasagna.

How to Make Easy Lasagna

  1. Cook and drain the ground beef, then stir in the spaghetti sauce and simmer.
  2. Combine the cottage cheese, 2 cups of mozzarella, eggs, half of the Parmesan, and seasonings.
  3. Assemble the lasagna according to the detailed recipe.
  4. ake, covered, for 45 minutes.
  5. Uncover and continue baking for 10 minutes.
